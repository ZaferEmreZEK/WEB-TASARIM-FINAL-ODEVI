After the 25% limit on house rents... Will it also apply to shops?
A striking statement! Pointing to canteens


Following the limitation of the increase rate in residential rents to 25%, there has been a notable statement regarding shop rents. Bendevi Palandöken, the President of the Confederation of Turkish Tradesmen and Craftsmen (TESK), demanded that the increase rate in shop and workplace rents be limited, similar to residential rents. Palandöken also mentioned canteen rents and said, "The Ministry of National Education has limited canteen rents by 25%."

While the limitation of the increase rate in residential rents to 25% was implemented, no such limitation was applied to shop and workplace rents. However, after a statement regarding this issue, the question of "Will there be a 25% limit on shop rents?" arose. The request for limiting the increase rate in shop rents came from Bendevi Palandöken, the President of TESK. In his written statement, Palandöken emphasized that the increase rates in shop and workplace rents have burdened the tradesmen.
