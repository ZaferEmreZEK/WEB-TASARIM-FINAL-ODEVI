Gold outperforms by 3 times! Its one-year return exceeds 200%: Experiencing high demand


Citizens turned to investment instruments against inflation. Lands, which are in high demand, have provided investors with returns of over 200% in one year. An average land in Istanbul has yielded 3 times more return than gold in annual terms. Lands in the northern regions of Istanbul, Çatalca, Silivri, Çanakkale, Yalova, Sakarya, İznik, and the western parts of Central Anatolia are experiencing high demand.

Lands, which are considered as long-term investments, have recently become part of short-term investment portfolios as they have appreciated by over 200% annually. Lands in the northern regions of Istanbul, Çatalca, Silivri, Çanakkale, Yalova, Sakarya, İznik, and the western parts of Central Anatolia are experiencing high demand. The increase in demand brings along price increases. In the past year, price changes in lands with zoning and agricultural status have reached rates of 200%.
