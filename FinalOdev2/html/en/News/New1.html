Dollar 24 lira, Euro 25 lira...Google data confuses users


Google, which showed the dollar exchange rate as 24 lira on October 29, surprised its users again today with the dollar and euro rates. Users who queried the dollar and euro rates on Google in the evening hours wondered about the reason behind the number they saw.

Due to the increase in recent years, the dollar and euro exchange rates are among the most searched questions on Google. However, users who want to learn the constantly changing exchange rates from Google were once again surprised.

As of November 5th, Sunday, citizens who wanted to learn the dollar exchange rate from Google encountered the result of 24.14 lira. When "Euro exchange rate" was searched, a similar result of 25.91 was displayed.
