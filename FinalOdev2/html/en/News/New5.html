EYT ile banka promosyonunda çıta yükseldi!
Promosyonda rekor rakam


One of the most talked about topics of recent times was EYT. While EYT retirees were waiting for the law to pass in the Parliament, bank promotions were also being researched. After the 30% increase in retirement pensions, retirees are looking for an answer to the question "How much is the bank promotion?". It was stated that banks will open their wallets and raise the bar in promotions with the EYT. It was previously known that retirees received 5,000 TL and 7,500 TL as promotions. Now, it is stated that the bar will be raised to 20,000 TL.

After the 30% increase in retirement pensions, bank promotions are on the agenda of retirees. Especially after the announcement of the decision regarding EYT, which millions of citizens have been eagerly waiting for, the question "How much is the bank promotion?" has become one of the most researched topics. Now, EYT retirees are waiting for the law to pass in the Parliament. During this process, banks have started to update their tariffs for promotions. According to the latest information, it is stated that banks are preparing to offer promotions up to 20,000 TL with the inclusion of EYT retirees in the retirees' caravan.
