Talep toplama tarihleri 7-8 December:
Sur Holiday Homes REIT going public


Sur Tatil Evleri REIT, operating under the umbrella of Sur Construction, has announced the dates for its initial public offering. The demand collection period for the IPO, led by Ziraat Investment Securities Inc. and co-led by Integral Investment Securities Inc., will take place on December 7-8 at a fixed price of 49.18 TL. Z. Altan Elmas, Chairman of Sur Construction, said, "With over 30 years of experience, we are taking a step into the capital markets."

Sur Tatil Evleri REIT, a rising value of Sur Construction, is going public. With over 30 years of expertise in real estate, shopping malls, retail, and energy sectors, Sur Construction is entering the capital markets with Sur Tatil Evleri REIT, which has a portfolio size of 9.8 billion TL. The IPO of Sur Tatil Evleri REIT, which is included in Sur Construction's tourism investments and approved by the Capital Markets Board (CMB), will be conducted through a consortium led by Ziraat Investment Securities Inc. and co-led by Integral Investment Securities Inc., with the demand collection period taking place on December 7-8.
